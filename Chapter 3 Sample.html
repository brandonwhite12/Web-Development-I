How to cook salmon

Salmon is a quick easy food when cooked in a skillet.

Ingredients

  • 1 6oz salmon fillet
  • 1tbs butter
  • 1tsp salt
  • 1tsp black pepper

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Once pan is hot lay salmon skin side down
  3. After skin begins to peel away from pan and salmon shows color, flip
  4. After searing other side, remove from pan and serve
